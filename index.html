The code calls for both of the div's to be the same height and width, but the 2nd div is larger because I use margin and padding in the CSS.

The total width of the element is the sum of the following: width + padding-left + padding-right + border-left + border-right + margin-left + margin-right, OR

250 + 10 + 10 + 5 + 5 + 10 + 10 = 300

The same holds for the height.

height + padding-top + padding-bottom + border-top + border-bottom + margin-top + margin-bottom, OR

100 + 10 + 10 + 5 + 5 + 10 + 10 = 150
